about us

Sri Aurobindo Institute of Technology (SAIT) is one of Indore’s well-known engineering colleges offering undergraduate programs in fields like Computer Science, IT, Mechanical, Civil and Electronics. The institute focuses on quality technical education, practical learning and all-round student development.
Students are encouraged to explore innovation, research, leadership and real-world problem solving through clubs, events and industry projects.
